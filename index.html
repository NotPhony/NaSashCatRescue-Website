A little about us: 

We are a non-profit cat rescue located in Melbourne’s Central Business District. We have many cats and kittens up for adoption. Most of which are rescued from the pound and taken in by us until they find their new loving homes. All cats and kittens are desexed, vaccinated, microchipped, de-wormed, and flea treated. We raise money through donations and government grants.

We are a cat exclusive organisation and don’t accept any other animals. We only accept cats because we would be overwhelmed by the amount of animals otherwise.
